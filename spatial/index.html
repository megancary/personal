About me

i'm talented, just ask my mom

Currently I live in Bowling Green, Kentucky. I work part-time as an intern at Williams and Associates Architects, a local architecture company ownerd by John Williams. I've worked with them to help design buisness and housing plans and enjoy every part of the design process. I also play bass guitar in my schools jazz band, as well as the french horn in concert settings.

I like people, places, and things. I also enjoy longboarding, and making people question my sanity.

I start school at Western Kentucky University in the Fall 2016 semester. I'll be working on my Bachelor's Degree in the Visual Arts with a concentration in Graphic Design.
